## 🎯 THE 6 RETAIL BANKING CATEGORIES

### 1. **ACCOUNTS & PAYMENTS**
Core transaction infrastructure, checking/savings accounts, P2P payments, bill pay, overdrafts, interest-bearing products.

**Key Benchmark Metrics:**
- Account opening speed (target: <5 minutes digital)
- Multi-currency support (target: 10+ currencies)
- Real-time settlement (target: 24/7 including weekends)
- Interest rate competitiveness vs. traditional banks
- Overdraft pricing & Sharia compliance

### 2. **CARDS**
Debit cards, credit cards (if Sharia permits), prepaid cards, virtual cards, multi-currency cards.

**Key Benchmark Metrics:**
- Physical + virtual card issuance speed
- Contactless/NFC capability
- Multi-currency card limits
- FX rates and markup
- Card control features (spend limits, merchant blocking)
- Reward structures

### 3. **INVESTMENTS**
Equities (stocks, IPOs, fractional shares), ETFs, bonds, digital gold, commodities, SIPs (systematic investment plans).

**Key Benchmark Metrics:**
- Asset class breadth (target: 1,000+ instruments minimum)
- Minimum investment (target: <AED 100 / $27)
- Fractional share availability
- Fee structure (commission-free preferred)
- Execution speed (real-time vs. delayed)
- Sharia screening methodology

### 4. **MORTGAGES**
Home purchase financing, Islamic mortgage products (Ijarah, Murabaha), refinancing, pre-approval digital processes.

**Key Benchmark Metrics:**
- Digital pre-qualification completion speed
- Full digital mortgage closure capability
- Interest/profit rate competitiveness
- LTV ratios offered
- Property valuation automation
- Regulatory compliance (DFSA, ECB guidelines)

### 5. **PERSONAL LENDING**
Personal loans, auto loans, buy-now-pay-later (BNPL), Islamic lending products (Qard Hassan, Murabaha), credit decisioning speed.

**Key Benchmark Metrics:**
- Credit decision speed (<5 seconds target)
- APR/profit rate competitiveness
- Credit score requirement (lower = inclusive)
- Loan amounts offered
- Repayment flexibility
- Late payment Sharia compliance

### 6. **EMBEDDED FINANCE (Lifestyle Integration)**
Embedded lending/payments within third-party platforms. Sub-categories:
- **Education**: Tuition financing, student loans, educational content integration
- **Travel**: Flight/hotel booking with payment options, travel insurance, forex
- **Health**: Medical payments, insurance, health data integration
- **Utilities**: Bill pay integration, escrow services
- **Maintenance**: Subscription management, warranty services
- **Other**: Insurance products, gift cards, rewards integration

**Key Benchmark Metrics:**
- Partner ecosystem size
- Embedded lending availability
- White-label capabilities
- API infrastructure maturity
- Regulatory sandbox approval

## ✅ SHARIA COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK

### 8 Core Islamic Financing Structures
1. **Murabaha** (cost-plus financing) → Personal loans, mortgages
2. **Musharaka** (equity partnership) → Investment products
3. **Mudaraba** (profit-sharing) → Asset management, deposits
4. **Ijarah** (lease) → Auto financing, real estate
5. **Istisna'a** (construction) → Mortgages, equipment financing
6. **Wakala** (agency) → Fund management, payments
7. **Qard Hassan** (benevolent loan) → Emergency lending
8. **Sukuk** (Islamic bonds) → Fixed income investments

### Governance Requirement
- **Sharia Supervisory Board** mandatory (3-5 scholars, quarterly reviews)
- **AAOIFI Standards** alignment required
- **Product-level fatwa** documentation for each financial product

### Embedded Finance Sharia Considerations
- Educational financing: Must avoid usury (Riba)
- Health financing: Permitted if non-interest-bearing
- Travel financing: Allowed with Sharia structures
- Utilities: Straightforward Qard Hassan model
- Subscription management: Asset-backed (Ijarah) preferred

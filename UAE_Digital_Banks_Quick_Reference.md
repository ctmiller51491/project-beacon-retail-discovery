## Pricing Tier Breakdown

### Free/Entry-Level Tier
```
LIV:       Goal Account (no fees, no minimum)
FAB:       iSave Account (no fees, no minimum, 4% interest)
ADIB:      Standard accounts (no explicit monthly fee)
MASHREQ:   NEO with Salary AED 5,000+ OR Balance AED 3,000+
```

### Standard Tier
```
LIV:       Primary/Silver/Gold Account (fee varies)
FAB:       Personal Current (AED 25 if balance < AED 3,000)
ADIB:      Ghina Savings (prize-linked with grand prizes)
MASHREQ:   NEO Standard (AED 26.25 fall-below fee)
```

### Premium Tier
```
LIV:       Max Subscription + Millionaire Account
FAB:       Elite Accounts + Etihad Guest Account
ADIB:      N/A (standard = premium for Islamic banking)
MASHREQ:   NEO PLUS (Salary AED 10,000+ OR Balance AED 50,000+)
```

### Ultra-Premium
```
LIV:       Not defined
FAB:       Private Banking
ADIB:      N/A
MASHREQ:   Mashreq Gold (AED 1,000+ quarterly + VAT)
```

```
LIV:       Free to AED 700 (depending on card)
```

## Key Differentiators

### Liv
- **Unique**: Digital gold, IPO trading, joint accounts
- **Best for**: Investment-focused digitally native customers
- **Risk**: Not Sharia-compliant

### FAB Digital
- **Unique**: Fully digital mortgages, Islamic banking division, virtual business cards
- **Best for**: Homebuyers and dual-option seekers
- **Strength**: Highest investment in digital mortgage infrastructure

### ADIB
- **Unique**: 100% Islamic, Amwali youth banking, prize-linked savings (Ghina)
- **Best for**: Practicing Muslims and youth
- **Strength**: Highest Islamic compliance rating

### Mashreq NEO
- **Unique**: US stock access, strong classic app (4.9/5), zero-fee remittances
- **Best for**: Salary earners and international remitters
- **Challenge**: NEO platform itself rated lower (3.8-4.1/5)

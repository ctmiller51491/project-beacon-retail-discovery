Build and launch L'Imad's digital banking platform in **4 waves** from March 2027 through 2030, targeting **1M+ affluent retail customers**, capturing **25-30% market share**, and generating **AED 200-250M annual revenue** by Year 3.

- **Competitive Advantage:** Islamic digital banking + UX/UI differentiation + embedded finance ecosystem

### **The Investment**
- **Total Effort:** 16,100+ person-hours (3-year roadmap)
- **Peak Team:** 100+ FTE by Wave 3
- **Capital:** Sovereign backing enables loss-leader rates and partnerships
- **Timeline:** Phased delivery allows validation and course correction

- **Year 1:** 100K customers, AED 500M AUM, 4.7★ app rating, AED 20-30M revenue
- **Year 2:** 350K customers, AED 2.5B AUM, 4.8★ app rating, AED 100-120M revenue
- **Year 3:** 1M customers, AED 8-10B AUM, 4.9★ app rating, **AED 200-250M revenue** (profitable)

**Profitability Path:** Year 3 achieves 15-20% ROAE through embedded finance (40% of revenue)

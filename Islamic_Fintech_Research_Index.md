## Glossary and Definitions

**Full Glossary**: Document 2, Appendix

**Key Terms Summary**:
- **Riba**: Interest/usury (prohibited)
- **Gharar**: Excessive uncertainty (prohibited)
- **Maysir**: Gambling/speculation (prohibited)
- **Wadia**: Safekeeping account
- **Mudaraba**: Profit-sharing partnership
- **Musharaka**: Joint venture
- **Murabaha**: Cost-plus sale
- **Ijarah**: Leasing
- **Istisna'a**: Manufacturing/construction finance
- **Sukuk**: Islamic bonds
- **Sharia**: Islamic law
- **AAOIFI**: International Islamic finance standards organization
- **SSB**: Sharia Supervisory Board

See Document 2 Appendix for complete definitions and applications.

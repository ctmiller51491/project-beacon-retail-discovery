## 14. RECOMMENDATIONS BY USE CASE

### For Individual Users

**Use Case: Global Traveler**
- Primary: Wise (multi-currency, low FX fees)
- Secondary: Revolut (more currencies, broader features)

**Use Case: US Gig Worker**
- Primary: Chime (early paycheck, SpotMe)
- Secondary: Square Cash/PayPal

**Use Case: Serious Investor**
- Primary: Revolut (Invest app) or Nubank (if in Brazil)
- Secondary: Interactive Brokers (if investment-focused)

**Use Case: LATAM Resident**
- Primary: Nubank (market leader, feature depth)
- Secondary: Regional alternatives

**Use Case: EU-Based Professional**
- Primary: Revolut or Monzo (feature depth)
- Secondary: Bunq (multi-IBAN) or Starling (SME)

**Use Case: Cost-Conscious Basic Banking**
- Primary: N26 or Monzo (free tier quality)
- Secondary: Wise (if multi-currency needed)

### For Merchants & SMEs

**Use Case: E-commerce Integration**
- BNPL: Nubank NuPay or Klarna
- Payments: Wise or Revolut
- Full platform: Grab (if SE Asia)

**Use Case: Payroll Integration**
- US SME: Chime integration
- EU SME: Starling Business
- LATAM: Nubank Business

**Use Case: Developer/Fintech**
- API platform: Wise Platform or Plaid
- BaaS: Multiple options
- Embedded: Wise Platform (strongest)

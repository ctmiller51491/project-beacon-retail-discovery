## THE OPPORTUNITY

### Market Size (Hong Kong)
- **2024:** HK$150-200M TAM → **2029:** HK$300-400M TAM (15-19% CAGR)
- **Crisis Level:** 49% burnout rate, 77% work stress, only 16% seek help
- **Gap:** Only 3.5% health spending on mental health (vs 10% international standard)

### Your Unique Angle
**"15-Minute Mental Health Sessions for Hong Kong's Time-Poor Professionals"**
- Competitors (Intellect, ThoughtFull): 45-60 min sessions, expensive
- You: 15-min micro-sessions, lower price, removes "I don't have time" objection
- Proven engagement: 20-45% (digital) vs 1-5% (traditional EAPs)

---

## THE BUSINESS MODEL

### B2B2C Structure
- **Sell to:** Employers (50-200 employee departments initially)
- **Serve:** Their employees (free/subsidized access)
- **Pricing:** HK$3,000 per employee per year (pilot), HK$3,200-3,500 (scale)
- **Minimum:** 50 employees or HK$150K annual contract

### What You're Selling
1. **15-minute live sessions** (video/voice) with qualified coaches/therapists
2. **10-module content hub** (stress, sleep, anxiety, resilience, etc.)
3. **HR dashboard** (engagement metrics, NPS, ROI reporting)
4. **Safety protocols** (crisis escalation, emergency contacts)

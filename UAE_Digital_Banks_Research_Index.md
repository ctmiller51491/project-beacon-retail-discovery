## FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Which bank should I choose?
**A**: Depends on your needs. See "Customer Segment Match Matrix" in Quick Reference or "Decision Framework" in Quick Reference document.

### Q: Which bank is best for Islamic banking?
**A**: ADIB (100% Sharia-compliant). See "Islamic Compliance Status" table in Quick Reference.

### Q: Which bank has the best app?
**A**: Mashreq UAE classic app (4.9/5). See "App Store Ratings" table in Quick Reference.

### Q: Can I invest in stocks?
**A**: Yes. Liv (UAE equities, IPOs), Mashreq NEO (US stocks), FAB (global funds). See Investment Matrix.

### Q: Do these banks offer mortgages?
**A**: FAB Digital (most comprehensive, fully digital), ADIB (3.99% fixed rate), Liv and Mashreq (limited). See Main Report.

### Q: What's the minimum to open an account?
**A**: Zero or very low. Most have no-minimum accounts. See Account Comparison Table.

### Q: Are there accounts with no fees?
**A**: Yes. Liv Goal, FAB iSave, ADIB (standard), Mashreq NEO (with conditions). See Pricing Breakdown.

### Q: Which bank targets youth?
**A**: ADIB (Amwali 8-18), Mashreq NEO (NEO NXT 8-18), Liv (Liv Lite 8-18). See Youth Banking Table.

### Q: Can I get personal loans?
**A**: Yes, all banks offer personal loans. See Lending Comparison in Quick Reference.

### Q: What are the app download ratings?
**A**: See App Store Ratings Table (Mashreq UAE highest at 4.9/5).

### Q: How is EmCare different?
**A**: EmCare is NOT a digital bank - it's healthcare services. Excluded from comparison.
